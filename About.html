Characters

Ace

Main character and was stuck in a jungle after running away from his hometown angry. He becomes friends with many others in the jungle, and they also help him learn new ideas

Geno Purdy

He is a very powerful character and has many magical powers, like controlling water and moving leaves place to place. He becomes Ace's best friend and shares a cabin with him.

Cheetah

Cheetah is his nickname. They call him that since he is really fast. Since he is really fast he goes and collects all the supplies from outside the jungle and comes back in a matter of no time.

Plastic Man

Plastic Man

Knockoff vesion of Iron Man, lives in the center of the jungle. He acts like he has powers but doesn't. He is the least liked in the jungle for the way he acts. He ran into the jungle because nobody appreciated him.

Banana kid

He is a teenager and is just learning about his many powers including summoning bananas
