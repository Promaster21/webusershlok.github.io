SpongeBob SquarePants by Nickelodeon

Overview

SpongeBob Squarepants is an exciting TV show where the main character, Spongebob, works at the Krusty Krab, makes new friends, and enjoys his life underwater. He works at the Krusty Krab with Mr. Krabs (his boss) and Squidward (his neighbor). Squidward and Patrick are Spongebob's neighbors and two best friends even though Squidward doesn't believe that.Spongebob also is friends with Sandy who loves to do karate with Spongebob.
