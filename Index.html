What is the purpose of Adventure in the Jungle?

The purpose of the show is to entertain the kids who enjoy watching TV and like adventure. Kids over 3 can watch Adventure in the Jungle. If kids don't like adventure and prefer something else, then the kids also get to view how life is in a jungle and people act. In the show the characters feelings are also shown, showing kids emotions people have when dealing with problems or solutions.
